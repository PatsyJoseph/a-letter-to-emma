You worked for this with everything you had, and I couldn't be prouder of you. I saw your discipline, consistency, passion, and willingness to keep on fighting for your dreams no matter what.

Watching, loving, and being with you changed me. Not just academically, but everything in me as I see my flaws and weaknesses.

You reminded me what hard work with passion looks like. You reminded me that progress is made one step at a time. And because of that, I stood up and I started to fight for my dreams again. To be a better person and be a man that I dreamt of.

As I reach my graduation, I want you to know that you played a bigger role in that journey than you probably realize. The confidence that I slowly rebuilt, the goals that I am driven to chase, and the growth that I experienced in college were all influenced, in one way or another, by the example you set.

Thank you for inspiring me Love. Thank you for helping me believe in myself again.

I know you feel sad and discouraged that you don't have an award. But Love, my graduation, growth and achievements were shaped by your example. My success I've worked so hard for is yours too. My Bronze Medals are not mine, it is ours. It's yours, Mama's, and mine.

I hope life continues to open doors for you. Whatever opportunities come your way, I hope you take them with pride and confidence. Be proud and confident! The world is better because of people like you. People who work hard, care deeply, and refuse to give up!

Congratulations sa imo Love! and congratulations on everything you've become. I will always be proud of you!

And now, as I finally walk to the stage, same as you walked to receive your diploma, I can finally say: We made it!

Thank you for everything Love! I am not here without you. I love you!

With love and appreciation,

Pat
